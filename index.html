About

Learning design with purpose.

I am an instructional designer focused on technical training, simulation-based learning, and the practical integration of generative artificial intelligence into various workflows. I have a Master's Degree in Modeling and Simulation with a focus on education and training, an undergrad in Psychology, and about a decade of experience creating learning solutions for the security and defense industries.

My background combines instructional systems design with experience developing training for complex systems. Over the course of my career, I have worked with engineers, subject matter experts, technical writers, and multi-disciplinary development teams to design curriculum, simulations, and interactive courseware supporting highly technical environments.

Though my professional career has always focused on adult learning, I have a passion for education in all forms and am constantly brainstorming ways to motivate my two young ones to engage with learning in fun and meaningful ways. My interests include:

  • Games and gamification
  • Music and art
  • Sports and outdoor activities
